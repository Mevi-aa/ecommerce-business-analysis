# E-Commerce Business Analysis

## Objective
Analyze revenue growth, product performance, customer behavior, and delivery operations to assess business sustainability.

## Tools Used
- SQL (PostgreSQL)
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Insights
- Revenue growth was driven primarily by increased order volume rather than higher average order value.
- A small number of product categories contributed most of the revenue, while some high-volume categories posed margin risks.
- Repeat buyers generated a disproportionate share of revenue compared to one-time buyers.
- Delivery delays were noticeable and may impact customer retention.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on customer retention strategies to increase lifetime value.
- Promote premium product categories and bundled offerings.
- Improve logistics efficiency to reduce delivery delays.
